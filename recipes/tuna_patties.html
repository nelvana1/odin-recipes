Tuna Patties

Description

Quick and easy lunch.Only handful of ingredients,for tasty and delicious meal.Or as i like to say:"Best flavor to effort ratio."

Ingredients

  • 1 Red onion
  • Can of tuna
  • 1 egg
  • Bread crumbs
  • Big spoon of mayo
  • Seasoning

Steps

  1. Cut the onion to small cubes.
  2. Get a medium sized bowl and mix your tuna,onion,egg.
  3. Add breadcrumbs and mayo.
  4. Mix and adjust consistency if needed.
  5. Add salt,pepper and seasons of your liking.
  6. Put your pan on medium heat,and add a touch of olive oil.
  7. Form a patties sized as you desire and cook them on both sides til they turn golden brown.
  8. Let them cool down on a paper towel and enjoy them as you like.I really like them with Sriracha and rice as side.

Chicken-Rice

Description

Simple after workout food.Not much of a gourmet,but gets the job done.

Ingredients

  • 250g of rice
  • 500g of chicken breast
  • 3 eggs
  • Seasonings

Steps

  1. Boil the rice.
  2. Cut chicken to nice bite size cubes.
  3. Add oil to pan and cook chicken on medium heat till its golden.
  4. Add rice and eggs to pan.
  5. Mix them on medium heat till eggs are cooked.
  6. Add salt,pepper and seasonings of your liking.
  7. Serve with something green sprinkled on top to look like a real dish.
  8. When feeling generous put little bit of sweet-chilli sauce on top for the will to live.
